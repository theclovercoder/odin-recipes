Chai Snickerdoodle Cookies

Description

It's sunny out and the family is home; why not whip out this recipe and make the family happy? Fast, easy, tasty, and sweet with a kick of spice.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¾ cups white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons chai concentrate

eps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat 1 3/4 cups sugar, butter, eggs, and chai concentrate together in a bowl until light and fluffy. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt together in another bowl. Stir flour mixture into butter mixture until dough is combined.
  3. Whisk 3 tablespoons sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl.
  4. Form dough into 1-inch balls and roll each ball in cinnamon sugar to coat; place 2-inches apart on baking sheets..
  5. Bake cookies in the preheated oven until golden, about 10 minutes. Cool on wire racks.
